Stake tokens — earn ONE every day

OTB Staking is inspired by HEX. You burn ONE to get OTB (1:1 on the Pools tab), then lock OTB for 400–10,000 days. When you stake, OTB is burned and you receive T-Shares — your claim on daily OTB inflation.

Every day, 3.69% of the effective OTB supply (real + virtual) is distributed proportionally to all T-Share holders. Longer stakes earn a duration bonus (up to 2× at 10,000 days), and the Adoption Amplifier gives early stakers up to 20% bonus T-Shares — fading to 0 at Day 1,825.

End your stake on time to receive your principal back plus all inflation rewards. Early exit incurs a penalty; late exit after a 14-day grace period also reduces your payout.

TOTALS — — —

The Bounty Prize Vault is a permissionless auction system. Anyone can lock any token (PLS, HEX, ERC-20s) as a prize for 1–100 days. Bidders compete using ONE tokens.

There are two ways to win: Instant Win — be the first to bid at or above the Bounty Price set by the depositor. Or Auction End — hold the highest bid when the timer runs out. Outbid users are refunded immediately.

The depositor receives 90% of the winning ONE bid. The remaining 10% is permanently burned, making ONE deflationary. Anti-snipe protection extends the timer by 15 minutes if a bid arrives in the last 10 minutes (max 3 extensions).
